Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O'Connell (/ˈaɪlɪʃ/ EYE-lish;[1] born December 18, 2001) is an American singer-songwriter. Her debut single "Ocean Eyes" went viral and garnered over 194 million views on Spotify, and the song "Bad guy" garnered all the major music awards, including an MTV EMA and a Grammy nomination.

The following list is a time line of Billie Eilish life

  • 2001 - Born in Los Angeles
  • 2006 - homeschooled and danced
  • 2009 - Started singing in a choir.
  • 2012 - Started writing and performing her own songs
  • 2015 - Recorded a first song «Ocean Eyes»
  • 2016 - Suffered an injury that forever interrupted her dancing career
  • 2017 - Presented her debut EP "Don't Smile at Me". The mini-album went gold, and one of the tracks on the album went even platinum.
  • 2018 - Went on her first tour called "Where's My Mind"
  • 2020 - Was nominated for a Grammy in six categories, including album of the year and song of the year.
